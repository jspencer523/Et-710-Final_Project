Artistic Education with Sophistication

Hello there! I go by the artist name Pensir and I'm a graphic designer, illustrator and author. Visual arts has been a passion of mine since early 2007 and that passion eventually lead me to study graphic design in Queensborough Community College in New York City. The art techniques I've been mostly creating with are pencil, ink and acrylic, but I'm comfortable creating art digitally as well. Alongside my college studies, I'm also working on an action-packed graphic novel about a military dictatorship titled "The Day with Hope."

As for now, I am still a student, which means that if you are looking to hire an artist to create visuals and you're on a strict budget, I may be able to help you.

Whatever your reason is for being here, I am glad you showed interest in my work, and if it caught your attention, I welcome you to follow my endeavor in the world of visual art!
